The Pharmacist Advantage in Your Coaching

My background as a pharmacist isn't just a credential; it's an integral part of how I coach. It allows me to:

  • Review potential interactions: Ensure your fitness and nutrition plan works safely alongside any medications you take.
  • Tailor plans for health conditions: Design effective programs considering conditions like diabetes, hypertension, arthritis, etc.
  • Understand physiological impacts: Apply a deeper knowledge of how exercise and diet affect your body at a biochemical level.
  • Provide credible information: Offer guidance grounded in science and health literacy.

This integrated approach provides an added layer of safety, efficacy, and personalization to your health journey.

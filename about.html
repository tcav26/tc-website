Meet Tim Cavanagh: Your Partner in Health

Bridging Pharmacy and Fitness

Welcome! I'm Tim Cavanagh, and my journey into health and wellness has taken a unique path, blending the scientific rigor of pharmacy with the practical application of fitness and nutrition coaching.

My fascination with how the human body works began early, leading me to pursue a career as a pharmacist. In practice, I saw firsthand the profound impact lifestyle choices have on long-term health, often complementing or even reducing the need for medication. However, I also noticed a gap: patients often received advice on diet and exercise but lacked the specific guidance and support to implement those changes effectively and safely, especially alongside their existing health conditions or treatments.

This realization sparked a second passion: fitness and nutrition. I became a certified fitness instructor to gain the expertise needed to bridge that gap. My mission is to empower individuals like you to take control of their health through sustainable, evidence-based fitness and nutrition strategies, informed by a deep understanding of health sciences.

I believe that optimal health comes from a holistic approach, considering every aspect of your well-being. Let's work together to build a healthier, stronger you.

Qualifications & Expertise

Pharmacy Background

  • Registered Pharmacist, PharmD, and Board Certified Pharmacotherapy Specialist
  • Over 10 years of experience as a practicing pharmacist.
  • Deep understanding of pharmacology, disease states, and medication management.
  • Expertise in identifying potential drug-nutrient and drug-exercise interactions.

Fitness & Nutrition Credentials

  • Certified Personal Trainer
  • Specialization in personalized diet and fitness plan building that fits your lifestyle.
  • Commitment to ongoing education in exercise science and nutrition.

My Coaching Philosophy

I believe in a client-centered, evidence-based approach. My goal isn't just to give you a plan, but to educate and empower you to make lasting changes. We'll focus on:

  • ✔Sustainability over quick fixes.
  • ✔Personalization based on your unique needs, goals, and health context.
  • ✔Building foundational habits for long-term success.
  • ✔Progress, not perfection, celebrating milestones along the way.
  • ✔Integrating fitness and nutrition seamlessly into your life.

Your health journey is unique, and your coaching should be too.
